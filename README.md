# Cakes4You

Psst... hungry ? Wanna grab some cake ? Is your mouth watering ?

Yes ?

Then this App is the exact thing you need right now !!

Here in Cakes4You you can order your lovely and sweet cakes that you want right at your doorsteps !!
Yes, you don't even need to step out of your house !!

Let it be a special occasion, birthday party or maybe just the wish to eat cakes, Cakes4You will bring your cake straight to you !!
